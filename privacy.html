Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

1. Introduction

At AskChad, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services, including our Application-to-Person (A2P) text messaging services.

By using AskChad's services, including subscribing to our text messaging program, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Text Messaging Data

When you opt in to receive text messages from AskChad, we collect:

  • Mobile phone number: To send you text messages
  • Opt-in consent records: Documentation of your consent to receive messages
  • Message content: Content of messages you send to us and we send to you
  • Usage data: Timestamp of messages, message delivery status, and frequency of communication
  • Device information: Mobile carrier information and device type

2.2 General Information

We may also collect:

  • Name and email address when provided through our contact forms
  • Business information for commercial inquiries
  • Website usage data through cookies and analytics tools
  • Communication preferences and feedback

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Text Messaging Purposes

We use your mobile phone number and related data to:

  • Transactional messages: Send service updates, appointment reminders, and account notifications
  • Marketing messages: Deliver promotional offers, product updates, and marketing content (only if you've opted in)
  • Customer support: Respond to your inquiries and provide assistance
  • Service improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance our messaging services
  • Compliance: Maintain records as required by telecommunications regulations

3.2 General Business Purposes

Your information may be used to:

  • Provide and maintain our AI sales automation services
  • Process your requests and communicate with you
  • Improve our products and develop new features
  • Protect against fraudulent or illegal activity

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

IMPORTANT: No Marketing or Sale of Text Messaging Data

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your text messaging opt-in data (including your mobile phone number and message content) with third parties for their marketing or promotional purposes. This prohibition is absolute.

4.1 Permitted Sharing for Service Provision

We may share your text messaging data ONLY with:

  • SMS Service Providers: Third-party messaging platforms and aggregators that facilitate the delivery of text messages (e.g., Twilio, bandwidth providers)
  • Technology Partners: Service providers who support our messaging infrastructure and security
  • Compliance Requirements: Government authorities when required by law or to protect our legal rights

These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and may only use it to perform services on our behalf.

4.2 General Privacy Policy Disclaimer

Please Note: While our general business practices may involve sharing certain types of information with business partners for analytics or service improvement, text messaging opt-in data is specifically EXCLUDED from any such sharing, except for the permitted service providers listed above who are essential to delivering text message services.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Secure storage of personal information with access controls
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Employee training on data protection and privacy practices
  • Compliance with telecommunications and data protection regulations

Important: While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we continuously work to maintain the highest standards of data protection.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Opt-Out of Text Messages

You can stop receiving text messages from AskChad at any time:

  • Text "STOP" to any message you receive from us. You will receive a confirmation message that you have been unsubscribed.
  • After texting STOP, you will no longer receive messages from us, except for a final confirmation message.
  • You may re-opt in at any time by following the original sign-up process.

6.2 Get Help

For assistance with our text messaging program:

  • Text "HELP" to any message you receive from us for assistance
  • Email us at privacy@askchad.net
  • Call us at (customer support number) during business hours

6.3 Message and Data Rates

Message frequency varies based on your preferences and the type of messages you've opted into. Standard message and data rates from your mobile carrier may apply. Please contact your mobile carrier for details on your messaging plan.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy
  • Comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes
  • Maintain opt-out records to honor your preferences

When you opt out of text messaging, we will retain your phone number on a suppression list to ensure we do not send you messages in the future, but we will delete associated message content and usage data in accordance with our retention policies.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately at privacy@askchad.net.

9. International Data Transfers

AskChad is based in the United States. If you access our services from outside the U.S., your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. By using our services, you consent to such transfers.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Post a prominent notice on our website
  • Send you a text message notification if the changes materially affect our text messaging practices (if you're subscribed)
  • Provide an email notification for significant changes (if we have your email)

Your continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
